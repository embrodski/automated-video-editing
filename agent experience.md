Notes on my experience using agents.

They really do know a lot of random stuff. For example, the correlation thing

They didn't seem to really know how to solve my problems

It would be very helpful if they were better at coming up with ways to test their work themselves.

Them losing all their state sometimes is pretty frustrating.

They seem to be kind of spaghetti-codey, where they don't really develop abstractions.

They seem to be happy to implement things that will obviously not satisfy the requirements.
